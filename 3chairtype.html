Tips

  • 3c type hair is prone to frizz, dryness, dullness, and damage.
  • When going through a wash day you must wash and condition thoroughly. You should choose a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that removes oils and grease without stripping the curl.
  • Since your hair is closer to the kinky side your hair needs a lot of moisture because it can become very dry.
  • After you use your regular conditioner you should do a deep condition such as Suave Professionals Coconut Oil Infusion Damage Repair Oil Treatment.
  • When choosing a deep conditioner make sure it won't weigh strands down while imparting much-needed slip.
