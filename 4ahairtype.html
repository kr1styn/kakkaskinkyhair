    Hair Tips

  • Hydration is the key for all types of natural hair. The kinkier your the more moisture you would need.
  • When your hair is kinky it is more prone to dryness and this will cause breakage so make sure you keep your hair moisturized
  • One good way to hydrate your hair is to deep condition your hair. Example : you can you use household items to make your conditioner like eggs, avocado, and mayo.
  • Make sure you detangle your hair because this will also cause breakage.
  • For sleep care to avoid frizz and breakage sleep with a silk scarf and or a silk pillowcase.
  • You should not sleep with a cotton scarf or pillowcase because it will cause your hair to become frizzy.
  • Just remember 4 type hair is the most delicate texture so you need to handle your hair with care and make sure maintain your hair since it is prone to fragileness.
