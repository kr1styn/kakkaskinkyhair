    Hair Tips

  • Moisturize your hair regularly. 4b is prone to be very dry which will cause a lot of breakage
  • Condition your hair every time to you wash to keep it moisturized.
  • Washing yiur hair in sections to prevent it from getting tangled, and it also helps to stretch your hair and keep your length.
  • Also yous should deep condition your hair at least twice a month.
  • For hair growth, protective styling is a key to get your hair to grow.
  • Protective styles like box braids, bantu knots, updos, and twists will help with less breakage
  • Trim your hair reguarly, if you don't then your ends will begin to split
